Shared Delegate Node for X-CASH(XCASH) DPOPS Voting

This website will give you

  • A dashboard that will show you how to vote for the shared delegate, the shared delegate fee, and how many blocks the delegate has found
  • Detailed list of the blocks found by the shared delegate
  • The amount of votes the delegate has, the public addresses that have voted for our delegate, with their reserve proofs
  • The payments and amoung of X-CASH the delegate has paid
  • The amount of X-CASH you have earned from staking with our delegate
